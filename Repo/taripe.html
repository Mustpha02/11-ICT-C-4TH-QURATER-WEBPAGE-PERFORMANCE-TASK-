About Me

Hi! I'm Reyana, but you can call me Rey or Nana — whichever feels right to you.

I’m the kind of person who always has pink on her nails, black in her wardrobe, and purple somewhere in my room. These three colors just feel like home to me.

I love things that are cute but a little moody — think Sanrio characters wearing goth makeup, iced lattes at midnight, playlists that go from Laufey to Chase Atlantic, and oversized hoodies on rainy days.

Little Things About Me

• I believe every outfit is better with at least one sparkly or shiny thing

• I screenshot conversations that make me laugh so I can reread them when I’m sad

• Night time is my most creative time (and also my most overthinking time)

• I collect pretty stickers, empty perfume bottles, and memories I probably romanticize too much
